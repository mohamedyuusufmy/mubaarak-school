Our History & Mission

Founded in 2001, Mubarak Primary School has been a pillar of quality education in Baledhawo. We are committed to providing a balanced education that combines academic excellence with moral values and practical skills.

Our mission is to nurture young minds through innovative teaching methods, state-of-the-art facilities, and a curriculum that prepares students for the challenges of the 21st century.

  • Qualified and experienced teachers
  • Modern teaching facilities
  • Holistic development programs
  • Safe and supportive environment
